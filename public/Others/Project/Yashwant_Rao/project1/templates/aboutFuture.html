    ABOUT
    FUTURE GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS

Future Group of Institutions is located at 18 km, Nh-24 Bareilly-Lucknow Road, near Faridpur, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh. The Future Group of Institutions operates under the auspices of Shree Rajendra Kumar Gupta Memorial Trust.


We at the Future University Bareilly believe that “The beautiful thing about learning is nobody can take it away from you!”.

We are also
FIET - Future Institute of Engineering and Technology
FIMS - Future Institute of Management and Studies
FOPFIMS - Faculty of Pharmacy Future Institute of Medical Sciences
FIMS - Future Institute of Medical Sciences
FIAMS - Future Institute of Ayurvedic Medical Sciences
FIP - Future Institute of Pharmacy

Technical Collaborations & Partnerships


We stress on quality education and produce industry-trained candidates. Collaborations are an essential part of professional courses. They bring us the much needed expertise and technological edge over other students.


Future College, which is one of top 10 colleges in U.P., has technical collaboration with IBM, where IBM imparts industrial training and certification to our B Tech and BCA students. A designated team from IBM provides training for all undergraduate students in engineering and computer applications.


Personality Development


Future College Bareilly organizes group learning activities for its students to improve their emotional intelligence. It helps turn an average student into a lifelong winner and industrial leader.


Faculty


At Future Institute, we cherry-pick industry-trained, highly qualified & experienced faculty members. Currently, we have 100+ faculty members in different departments comprising lecturers, professors, and assistant professors. Apart from that, we also invite renowned academicians as guest faculty from the leading institutions of India.


Campus


The sprawling campus spreads over an area of 30 acres on Bareilly-Lucknow highway in a naturally green & peaceful surroundings. It’s 18 km away from Satellite Bus Station, Bareilly.
The campus’s lush green environment, beautiful department buildings and state-of-the-art labs inspire young students to make the most of the learning opportunities.


Developing Corporate Leaders


As the term “future” itself suggests, Future Institute, one of top 10 colleges in Bareilly, believes in futuristic possibilities and developing future leaders.
At Future Institute, we emphasize stress-free learning, and you can only achieve it by creating an environment that stimulates creativity and fun learning.
Highly experienced faculty members keep themselves updated with latest industry trends and practices.
Future Institute’s education and learning systems help cultivate analytical and solution oriented thinking. It makes us one of the best ranked engineering colleges in India.


Alumni


We have a 12,000+ global alumni-base employed in the leading companies and organizations worldwide.
Needless to say that Future Institute is one of the best institutes for engineering in India. Our alumni work in more than 50 countries, many in Fortune 500 companies.


Education Methodology


We stress on experiential learning rather than text-book based knowledge. We provide our students extensive exposure to diverse fields through meticulously developed learning tools.
Apart from subject knowledge, we train our students in leadership development. In these training sessions, Future Institute puts them in situations that hone their decision-making skills.
These skill development sessions are highly beneficial in tackling real-life problems in the corporate sector.
