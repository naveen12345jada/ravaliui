ABOUT CLINIC APPOINMENT SYSTEM

A Clinic Appoinment System is element of health information that focuses mainly on the administrational needs of hospital.In many implementations.a PIS is a
comprehensive, intergrated informaation system designed to manage all the aspects of a hospital's operation,such as medical,administrative ,finacial,and legal issuse and the
corresponding processing of service. Patient Information System architecture has three main levels,Center Government Level, Territory Level, and Patient carrying Level
Generally,all types of hospital informaation system(PIS) are supported in client-server architecture for networking and processing, Most work positions for HIS are currently
resident types,Mobile computing began with wheeled PC stands.Now tablet computers and smartphone application are used
